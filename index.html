❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my password?
    Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.
  • How do I create a prescription?
    Navigate to Prescriptions from the dashboard, tap the + button, select a patient, and fill in the prescription details.
  • How do I add a new patient?
    Go to Patients from the dashboard, tap the + button, and enter the patient's details including ID number and contact information.
  • Is my data secure?
    Yes. EcoFlow uses industry-standard encryption, secure authentication, and complies with POPIA requirements for protecting personal and health information.
  • Who can use EcoFlow?
    EcoFlow is designed for registered healthcare professionals, practice managers, and authorised staff involved in homecare oxygen services.
